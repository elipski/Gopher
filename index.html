About The Typeface

Gopher is an sans serif typeface that can be both thick or thin depending on the weights used with it. It is built and looks geometric with is reverse contrast and thicker vertical and thinner horizontal strokes. Due to both its characteristics and the year it was created, Gopher is a modern typeface and has become increasingly popular since 2019.

Adam Ladd Foundry

Created by Adam Ladd

This typeface was created in 2019 in three styles. It was made to be unique while also remaining familiar. By having different weight options, Gopher works well for both body and display texts. It was created for designs seen in branding, fashion, magazines, and many others. The weights between the horizontal and vertical strokes was flipped by Ladd to create its unique look.
